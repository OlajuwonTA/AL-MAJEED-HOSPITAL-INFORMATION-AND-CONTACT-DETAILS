About Al-Majeed Hospital


A beacon of hope and healing, built on a foundation of compassion, personal loss, and an unshakable commitment to quality healthcare for all.


Founded by: Pharmacist Muslihudeen Tijani Adekilekun, a UK-based pharmacist and philanthropist, the hospital was born out of a tragic but transformative event — the untimely death of our beloved brother, Majeed Tijani Adekilekun.Majeed lost his life due to the absence of competent and quality medical care when he needed it most. This heartbreaking loss ignited a passion in our to ensure that no one else in the community and beyond would have to suffer such a fate — not from lack of care, nor from inadequate facilities.


Al-Majeed Hospital stands as a living tribute to Majeed’s memory — a place where every life matters, and every patient is treated with dignity, urgency, and expert care.


Situated in a serene environment at Ede, Osun State, the hospital is led by Dr. Musbau Olaniran, our trusted Lead Medical Officer, and supported by a dedicated team of skilled doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and healthcare professionals. Together, we offer world-class medical care with a human touch.


Our Mission
To provide exceptional, patient-focused healthcare services through modern facilities, skilled professionals, and a compassionate approach — ensuring that no one is left behind in moments of medical need.

Our Vision
To become a trusted healthcare institution in Nigeria — a symbol of excellence, compassion, and innovation — inspired by personal loss and driven by the belief that every life is worth saving.


Why Al-Majeed Hospital Stands Out
  • Built on Purpose and Passion: Inspired by a profound personal story, every corner of this hospital carries a deep commitment to human life and dignity.

  • World-Class Facilities: We offer advanced diagnostics, inpatient and outpatient services, and emergency care using up-to-date medical technology.

  • 24/7 Power Supply: Our facility is powered round-the-clock to ensure seamless care and comfort for every patient.

  • Complete Surveillance: Our CCTV systems and security protocols guarantee a safe environment for all.

  • In-House Pharmacy: Accessible, well-stocked, and managed by licensed pharmacists for faster and more reliable treatment.

  • Diagnostic Excellence: Timely and accurate results from modern equipment and trained laboratory professionals.

  • Professional, Empathetic Staff: Our team is not just trained — they are driven by compassion and patient respect.

  • Clean, Peaceful Environment: Designed to promote wellness, recovery, and peace of mind for patients and families.

At Al-Majeed Hospital, we don’t just treat illnesses — we honor lives. From the legacy of one life lost too soon, we have built a home for healing, where every patient is given the chance their loved ones hoped for.
